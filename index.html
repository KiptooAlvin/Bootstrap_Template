About WANFAM

WANFAM is a smart livestock and farm management platform designed to help farmers, farm managers, and livestock investors monitor, track, and improve farm productivity with real-time insights.

  • Centralized livestock database for record keeping.
  • Real-time health monitoring and disease alerts.
  • Feeding schedules and cost optimization.
  • Productivity trend analysis and forecasting.

WANFAM empowers farms to reduce losses, improve efficiency, and increase profitability.

Centralized Records

Maintain detailed animal and farm records for better management decisions.

Efficient Feeding

Plan feed schedules and track consumption to optimize costs.

Productivity Insights

Analyze growth, milk yield, breeding cycles and farm trends.

Farm Resource Management

Manage workers, equipment, and daily schedules effectively.
